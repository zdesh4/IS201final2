How the tech industry has changed in 2022

After seeing a boom in tech company employment during the 2020 Covid-19 pandemic, we are seeing a large wave of layoffs. With the economy's current state CEOs are realizing that it hurting their company to have as many employees as they do, so we are seeing executives starting to make changes and have been laying employees off at rapid rates.

Many well-known companies have had large amounts of layoffs this year, but some of the most popular companies that have been in the news lately are Meta, Amazon, Netflix, and Twitter. The data tab has a graph and a YouTube video that explains the data surrounding these layoffs in the tech industry.
